# About KiraSub

## The Problem
Recurring revenue and subscription management in the Web3 ecosystem is currently fragmented and high-friction. 
Merchants building decentralized applications face two difficult choices:
1. **Crypto-Native Complexity**: Force users into complex, single-chain payment flows that alienate mainstream audiences.
2. **Web2 Centralization**: Use traditional payment processors (like Stripe) but rely on centralized databases to manage access control, breaking the decentralized promise of Web3 applications.

## The Solution: KiraSub
**KiraSub** is a production-ready subscription engine built for Solana applications. It acts as a bridge between the seamless, multi-chain payment experience of Web2 and the verifiable, decentralized access control of Web3.

By serving as the "Stripe for Solana Entitlements," KiraSub allows merchants to:
* **Accept Global Payments**: Users can pay for subscriptions using any token on any supported chain (Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, etc.) or traditional fiat methods.
* **Automate On-Chain Access**: Upon successful payment, KiraSub automatically mints a verifiable **Entitlement PDA** (Program Derived Address) directly to the user's Solana wallet.
* **Manage with Ease**: Merchants get access to a premium, glassmorphic dashboard to track Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR), active subscribers, and manage subscription tiers.

## Core Value Proposition
KiraSub provides a frictionless checkout experience for the end-user regardless of what wallet or chain they prefer, while giving the merchant a decentralized, composable source of truth for access rights on the highly performant Solana network.
